About

Who is Katrin Skelton?

I am a freelance UX designer, an actor in improvisation theatre, a dreamer who realizes dreams, an urban lover and a person who likes to be inspired by the people I meet and the conversations we have.

Have you always been an arty type?

Being creative has always been my strength and I have always been a visual person. Studying Information Design took me to a new city and opened a new chapter in my life. I love to work on my own creative projects or as part of an interdisciplinary team.

What kind of design do you make?

As a full-stack designer I solve problems by thinking creatively as well as following the UCD process. My forte is to design online & offline products making them user-friendly, intuitive and joyful.

And what else?

When I am not working, I am at play in the arts. You can find me acting in the improvisation theatre, playing the harmonica, making my own crazy salt and sugar variations and browsing flea markets. Lately I have been writing and designing my own book.

Sendung mit der Maus

I conceived an experience-oriented concept for the website of the mouse
(’Die Seite der Maus‘). The page was designed with regard to children‘s development-psychology theories and focused on the ‘stimulation need’. There were factors of stimulation being used as a basis that may have had a significant influence on the UX design.

ROLE (UX RESEARCHER & UX DESIGNER)
I created and built new ideas for kids within scenario based design.
My responsibilities included: analysis, research, personas & szenarios | survey of the ‘stimulation need’ |developmental psychology of children | usability for children | scribbles | final mockups

  • Client: http://www.wdrmaus.de/
  • Date: 2014

My Hen's night, a Creative Book

The second most beautiful day (after marriage) must be celebrated and should be kept in memory. With this book the whole day will be recorded
pictorially and in written form.

The bride is leaded through the whole day by solving creative, hilarious and playful tasks. At the end of her amazing day she will know what kind of bride she is. Will she be a serious, resourceful or even an adventurous bride? The book delivers the answer.
