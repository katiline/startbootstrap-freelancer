About

As a full-stack designer I solve problems by thinking creatively. Prototyping the interaction concepts of holistic user interfaces and focusing on the user-centered design process using everything from scribbles to Axure or Balsamiq Mockups and Photoshop is my forte.

I design products to be user-friendly, intuitive and joyful. I love being creative and working in an inspiring work environment as part of an interdisciplinary team. When I am not UX-ing you can find me acting in the theatre, or browsing fleamarkets!

Sendung mit der Maus

I conceived an experience-oriented concept for the website of the mouse (’Die Seite der Maus‘). The page was designed with regard to children‘s development-psychology theories and focused on the ‘stimulation need’. There were factors of stimulation being used as a basis that may have had a significant influence on the UX design.

ROLE (UX RESEARCHER & UX DESIGNER)
I created and built new ideas for kids within scenario based design.
My responsibilities included: analysis, research, personas & szenarios | survey of the ‘stimulation need’ |developmental psychology of children | usability for children | scribbles | final mockups

  • Client: http://www.wdrmaus.de/
  • Date: 2014

'Mein JGA' Creative Book

With this project I realised one of my dream: My first own book. The second most beautiful day (after marriage) must be celebrated and should be kept in memory. With this incredible book the whole day will be recorded pictorially and in written form. The bride is leaded through the whole day by solving creative, hilarious and playful tasks. At the end of her amazing day she will know what kind of bride she is. Will she be a praiseworthy, cheeky or even a brilliant bride? The book delivers the answer. I was in charge of the whole book, concept, design and text included.
